Yingjie Li

Biography

Born in China, Yingjie Li is a self-taught artist. Holding a Ph.D in Biochemistry from Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey. Yingjie has lifelong interest in art and has been inspired to draw and paint since childhood. Yingjie is now living with her husband and two kids in West Chester, PA. She is a member of Chester County Art Association and has received awards and honors on her artwork.
